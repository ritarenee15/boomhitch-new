“You need more than a logo. You need a connective experience.” Creative, collaborative and a connecter at her core, Angela Prado is an entrepreneur and business owner who approaches design and branding from a universal perspective to drive results. She listens deeply and takes time to build relationships, which leads to high-quality marketing services and solutions for her clients.

Rooted in the belief that business is more than transactional, Angela runs Boomhitch from her heart. New clients become loyal customers and friends who come back to work with her to elevate their company’s brand experience. Her eye for different perspectives is influenced by a life lived in places such as Chile, Minnesota, Colorado, New York, and in and around Richmond, VA.
